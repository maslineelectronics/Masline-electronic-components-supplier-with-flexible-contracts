## Contract Types and Models

Flexible supply contracts are not one-size-fits-all. Each contract model supports specific manufacturing or supply chain objectives. A diverse set of contract types ensures that each company can align terms with its procurement goals.

### Common Contract Models
- **Blanket Purchase Contracts:** Define long-term pricing and delivery schedules.  
- **Consignment Stock Agreements:** Maintain inventory close to production sites.  
- **Vendor-Managed Inventory (VMI):** Supplier monitors and replenishes stock as needed.  
- **Fixed-Term Contracts:** Lock in prices and conditions for specific durations.  
- **Open Orders:** Provide the ability to modify quantities during the contract period.  

These contract types simplify component management while ensuring supply consistency.
